# Privacy Policy — Life Tracker (personal use)

This describes how **you** (the sole user) handle data when running this project.

- **Local storage:** Expense and health-related data are stored in a local SQLite database on your machine.
- **Third parties:** The bot may send messages to Google (Gemini), Telegram, Fitbit, and Gmail only as configured in your `.env` file.
- **No resale:** This personal build does not sell your data.

For questions, refer to your own deployment and the README in this repository.
